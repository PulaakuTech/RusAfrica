About Don State Technical University

Don State Technical University (DSTU), established in 1930, is one of the oldest and most prestigious technical universities in southern Russia. Located in the vibrant city of Rostov-on-Don, DSTU is a hub of scientific research, innovation, and advanced education. The university has played a crucial role in the industrial development of the region and continues to be at the forefront of training skilled professionals across various technical disciplines.

With a student population of over 40,000, DSTU offers a wide range of educational programs, including Bachelor's, Master's, and Doctoral degrees across engineering, computer science, architecture, robotics, economics, and humanities. The university collaborates with global institutions to ensure international standards in teaching and research, and provides double-degree and exchange programs with partner universities worldwide.

DSTU boasts a modern campus with state-of-the-art laboratories, research centers, and technology incubators. The university's academic staff includes renowned scientists, industry experts, and experienced professors dedicated to fostering a creative and inclusive learning environment.

The city of Rostov-on-Don, where DSTU is located, is a major educational, cultural, and economic center in southern Russia. With excellent infrastructure, affordable living, and a diverse community, it provides an ideal environment for international students seeking quality education in Russia.
